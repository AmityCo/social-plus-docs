## User Lifecycle Management

Understanding the complete user journey is crucial for building engaging social applications:

1. **User Creation**: Registration, authentication, and initial setup
2. **Profile Management**: Updating profiles, preferences, and settings
3. **Safety & Moderation**: Implementing community guidelines and safety measures
4. **User Retention**: Engagement strategies and user experience optimization

## Best Practices

- Implement comprehensive user verification processes
- Design clear content moderation policies
- Provide users with control over their privacy and safety settings
- Regularly monitor user engagement and community health metrics

## Getting Started

1. Begin with [User Operations](/social-plus-sdk/core-concepts/user-management/user) to understand user lifecycle management
2. Implement [Content Moderation](/social-plus-sdk/core-concepts/user-management/content-moderation) to maintain community standards

These user management concepts are fundamental to creating safe, engaging social experiences that scale with your community.
