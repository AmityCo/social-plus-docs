## üö¶ Getting Started

Ready to build amazing social experiences? Here's how to get started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Platform">
    Select your development platform and follow the installation guide:
    - [iOS Development](/social-plus-sdk/getting-started/platform-setup/mobile/install-ios)
    - [Android Development](/social-plus-sdk/getting-started/platform-setup/mobile/install-android)
    - [Web Development](/social-plus-sdk/getting-started/platform-setup/web/install-web)
    - [React Native](/social-plus-sdk/getting-started/platform-setup/cross-platform/install-react-native)
    - [Flutter](/social-plus-sdk/getting-started/platform-setup/cross-platform/install-flutter)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up Authentication">
    Configure your API keys and authentication system following our [authentication guide](/social-plus-sdk/getting-started/authentication).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Features">
    Decide which features you want to implement:
    - Start with [Chat & Messaging](/social-plus-sdk/chat/overview) for real-time communication
    - Add [Social Features](/social-plus-sdk/social/README) for feeds and interactions  
    - Include [Video Streaming](/social-plus-sdk/video-new/overview) for live content
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Build & Deploy">
    Use our comprehensive guides and examples to build your application, then deploy with confidence using our scalable infrastructure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
