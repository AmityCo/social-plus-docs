# Dashboard

Our dashboard offers comprehensive insights into the activity within your community, chat, and live stream. It provides detailed analytic data that enables you to gain a deeper understanding of your users' engagement behavior, empowering you to make informed decisions to enhance their experience.

## Enabling Social Insights

To enable the Social Insights feature, please contact our [<mark style="color:green;">support team</mark>](<mailto:support@social.plus >). Once the support team raises a task ticket, our development team will enable the feature for you.
