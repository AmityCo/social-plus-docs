## Console Logo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">Settings → Brand settings.</Step>
  <Step title="Upload">Select image (PNG/JPG, ≤10MB, ~500×200px recommended).</Step>
  <Step title="Preview">Verify scaling & background contrast.</Step>
  <Step title="Reset (Optional)">Use Reset to Default if reverting.</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>Use a horizontally oriented logo with transparent background when possible.</Info>

## Brand Account

A special account with verification badge for official posts & comments.

### Create Brand Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open">Settings → Brand settings → Brand account section.</Step>
  <Step title="Create">Click “Create new account”.</Step>
  <Step title="Configure">Upload logo (avatar) + enter display name (≤100 chars).</Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">Click Create account.</Step>
</Steps>

### Requirements

| Field | Purpose | Tips |
|-------|---------|------|
| Logo | Avatar identification | Use primary mark; test dark/light modes |
| Brand Name | Display name across posts/comments | Align with public trademark; avoid internal acronyms |

### Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Consistency">Single canonical brand account; no duplicates.</Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tone">Professional, clear, conflict‑neutral responses.</Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Access Control">Limit credential holders; rotate on role changes.</Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Separation">Do not mix personal opinions; keep to official stance.</Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Brand Changes">Announce ahead of logo/name alterations.</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Metrics

| Metric | Description | Action Trigger |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Brand Post Share (%) | Brand posts / total posts | Low reach → schedule consistent cadence |
| Response SLA (mins) | Time to first brand reply (support threads) | Rising → review staffing/access |
| Engagement Rate | Reactions + comments per brand post | Decline → adjust content strategy |
| Misuse Incidents | Unauthorized posts | Any >0 → tighten access |

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely Cause | Resolution |
|-------|--------------|-----------|
| Logo blurry | Low resolution asset | Upload higher-res within recommended ratio |
| Missing badge | Account not created as brand type | Recreate or contact support |
| Duplicate brand voice | Multiple unofficial accounts | Consolidate & communicate official handle |
| Unauthorized post | Credential sharing | Rotate credentials; implement SSO if possible |
